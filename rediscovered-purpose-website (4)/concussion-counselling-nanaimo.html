After a concussion, everyday life can feel overwhelming.

You might experience:

  • Light and sound sensitivity
  • Brain fog
  • Mental fatigue
  • Irritability

I provide concussion counselling in Nanaimo to help reduce overstimulation and support recovery.

How I Help

We focus on:

  • Calming the nervous system
  • Reducing overload
  • Building tolerance gradually

ICBC direct billing available. If your concussion resulted from a motor vehicle accident, I am a registered ICBC provider and can direct bill your claim. Learn more about ICBC Counselling Nanaimo. Contact me at davedyck@rediscoveredpurposecounselling.ca to confirm your coverage.
